# Start Here

> **Find your rung on the ladder in two minutes, then take the next concrete step.**

This site is a ladder, not a library. You don't have to read it front to back — you just need to find where you are now and take the next step. This page helps you do exactly that.

---

## The five stages, in one breath

| Stage | You are here if… | The shift |
|-------|------------------|-----------|
| **1 · Chat** | You have a Copilot license but mostly use it for quick questions and rewrites. | *Use it in the flow of work.* |
| **2 · First-Party Agents** | You're fluent in chat and want more, but didn't realize agents are already included. | *Use what's already built for you.* |
| **3 · Cowork** | You trust Copilot for single steps and want to hand off whole tasks. | *Let it run multi-step work.* |
| **4 · Agent Builder** | You keep repeating the same delegated task and think "I wish this just existed." | *Build a simple agent — no code.* |
| **5 · Copilot Studio** | Your simple agents are hitting limits — you need real knowledge, actions, and governance. | *Build production agents.* |

---

## Two-minute self-assessment

Answer honestly. Count the **last statement you can say "yes, comfortably" to** — that's roughly your current stage.

1. **Chat:** "I open Copilot in Teams, Word, or Outlook several times a week and get useful results."
2. **First-Party Agents:** "I've used at least one of the included agents — Researcher, Analyst, Facilitator, or another — on real work."
3. **Cowork:** "I've handed Copilot a multi-step task and let it work through it, not just one prompt."
4. **Agent Builder:** "I've built (or could build) a simple declarative agent over my own files or a SharePoint site."
5. **Copilot Studio:** "I've published an agent with a knowledge source and at least one real action."

**Where you landed → where to go:**

- **Stopped at 1** → [Stage 1 · Chat](stages/stage-1-chat.md). Start with [Turn a meeting into tracked follow-ups](walkthroughs/chat-meeting-followups.md).
- **Stopped at 2** → [Stage 2 · First-Party Agents](stages/stage-2-first-party.md). See [the agents already in your license](walkthroughs/first-party-agents-overview.md).
- **Stopped at 3** → [Stage 3 · Cowork](stages/stage-3-cowork.md). Try [handing off an end-to-end task](walkthroughs/cowork-end-to-end-task.md).
- **Stopped at 4** → [Stage 4 · Agent Builder](stages/stage-4-agent-builder.md). Build [a team-knowledge agent over SharePoint](walkthroughs/agent-builder-team-knowledge.md).
- **Stopped at 5** → [Stage 5 · Copilot Studio](stages/stage-5-studio.md). Build [your first Studio agent](walkthroughs/studio-first-agent.md).

You don't have to climb one rung at a time — but each stage makes the next one easier, so it pays to be honest about where you are.

## Reading by role

The journey is the spine of this site; your **role** is how you narrow within each stage. Most walkthroughs are tagged for one or more of these readers:

| Role | What you're usually after |
|------|---------------------------|
| **End user** | "Give me a prompt I can paste in right now." |
| **Champion / adoption lead** | "Give me use cases to show my team, plus a rollout path." |
| **Manager / team lead** | "What's the ROI story and what should my team try first?" |
| **Maker / power user** | "How do I go from *using* Copilot to *building* an agent?" |
| **IT / admin** | "What has to be in place before each stage works?" |

Use the search box (top of every page) or the **[Use-Case Catalog](CATALOG.md)** to filter by role and stage.
