# What can I do with Copilot in Word?

> **Read less, draft faster.** Pick the job in front of you and try one of these right now.

Copilot is built into Word, and the Microsoft 365 Copilot app can read and draft from your documents too. These are the highest-value moves for anyone who works in documents.
