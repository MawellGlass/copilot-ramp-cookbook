# What can I do with Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat?

> **The one place Copilot can see across all your work.** Pick the job in front of you and try one of these right now.

The Microsoft 365 Copilot app (and Copilot Chat) reaches across your email, files, meetings, and chats at once — so it's the right starting point whenever your question spans more than a single app. These are the highest-value moves.
