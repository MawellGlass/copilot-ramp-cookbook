# What can I do with Copilot in Teams?

> **Never lose a meeting outcome or a channel decision again.** Pick the job in front of you and try one of these right now.

Copilot is built into Teams meetings and chat — and the first-party agents extend it further inside channels and communities. These are the moves that pay off fastest.
