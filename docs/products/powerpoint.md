# What can I do with Copilot in PowerPoint?

> **From raw notes to a story you can present — without the blank-slide stare.** Pick the job in front of you and try one of these right now.

Copilot helps you turn content you already have — notes, a brief, a decision log — into a structured deck, then tailor it to the room. These are the best starting moves.
