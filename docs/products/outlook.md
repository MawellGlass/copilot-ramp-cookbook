# What can I do with Copilot in Outlook?

> **Your inbox and calendar, on autopilot.** Pick the job in front of you and try one of these right now — each is a paste-a-prompt walkthrough, no setup.

Copilot lives right inside Outlook (and in the Microsoft 365 Copilot app, which can read your mail and calendar too). These are the highest-value moves for anyone who spends their day in email.
