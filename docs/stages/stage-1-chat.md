# Stage 1 · Copilot Chat

> **Use Copilot in the apps you already live in. This is where the ramp starts — and where most of the everyday value is.**

**The shift:** from "I have a license" to "I reach for Copilot in the flow of work." No new tools, no setup — just the chat you already have in Teams, Word, Outlook, and the M365 Copilot app.

---

## When you're ready for this stage

You have a Microsoft 365 Copilot license and want quick wins that fit your existing day. Everything here is paste-a-prompt simple — no agents, no building.

## What you need

- A Microsoft 365 Copilot license.
- Copilot showing up in Teams, Word, Outlook, or the M365 Copilot app.
- Two minutes and a real task in front of you.
