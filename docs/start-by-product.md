# Start by Product — "What can I even do?"

> **Pick the app you already have open and the job in front of you. Get three things to try right now.**

Most people don't wake up thinking "I'd like to climb the AI maturity ladder." They think *"I'm in Outlook drowning in email — can Copilot help?"* This page starts from there: **your app, your job, three moves you can make in the next five minutes.**

!!! tip "Prefer a different entry point?"
    - Know your skill level? Use **[Start by Stage](start-here.md)** and its 2-minute self-check.
    - Want it grouped by your job? Use **[Start by Role](start-by-role.md)**.
