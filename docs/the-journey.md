# The Journey

Five stages. Each one builds on the last — but you can start wherever you are.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **Stage 1 · Copilot Chat**

    ---

    Build a daily habit in the apps you already use — Teams, Word, Outlook, the M365 Copilot app. No setup, no new tools: just prompts in the flow of work.

    **You're ready for this if** you have a Copilot license and want quick, paste-and-go wins.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Go to Stage 1](stages/stage-1-chat.md)

-   **Stage 2 · First-Party Agents**

    ---

    Researcher, Analyst, Facilitator, Interpreter — agents Microsoft already built and included in your license. Most people don't know they're there.

    **You're ready for this if** you're fluent in Chat and want more without building anything.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Go to Stage 2](stages/stage-2-first-party.md)

-   **Stage 3 · Cowork**

    ---

    Stop prompting step by step. Hand off whole multi-step tasks — a full deck, a weekly digest, a synthesis across a dozen documents — and let Copilot run them.

    **You're ready for this if** you trust Copilot for individual steps and want to delegate whole workflows.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Go to Stage 3](stages/stage-3-cowork.md)

-   **Stage 4 · Agent Builder**

    ---

    When you keep repeating the same Cowork task, turn it into a custom agent — over your files, your SharePoint site, your org's knowledge. No code required.

    **You're ready for this if** you've thought "I wish this just existed" more than once.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Go to Stage 4](stages/stage-4-agent-builder.md)

-   **Stage 5 · Copilot Studio**

    ---

    Your simple agents are hitting limits. Copilot Studio gives you real knowledge bases, external connectors, MCP tool integrations, and enterprise-grade governance.

    **You're ready for this if** your agents need to call APIs, go beyond SharePoint, or be governed at scale.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Go to Stage 5](stages/stage-5-studio.md)

</div>
